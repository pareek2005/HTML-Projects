How to Make Bread and Chutney Pudi

  1. Take out 4 bread slices
  2. Turn on the stove.
  3. Put oil on the bread slices.
  4. Place the bread slices on the stove.
  5. Let them sit until they turn slightly black.
  6. Take them out and liberally put chutney pudi on both sides of each slice.
